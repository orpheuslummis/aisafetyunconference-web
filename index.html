AI Safety Unconference 2018

Saturday December 8, 1200-1700
Location TBD, Montréal, QC

Description

The potential benefits and risks of AI technologies loom large on the future of the world. Creating safe AI, both in the near-term and long-term, is a multifaceted and complex endeavour. By bridging perspectives and coordinating better we can increase the likelihood of better outcomes.

The AI Safety Unconference focuses on bringing together interested people and giving them minimal structure, leaving as much space for meaningful exchange and exploration.

Structure of the event (5h): intro & lunch, 2h session, break, 2h session, closure.
